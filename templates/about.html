Who we are

Built on Legacy, Driven by Vision

Home > Who we are

Super Moda Real Estate was founded with a simple yet powerful goal: to redefine how real estate is experienced in Dubai. Backed by a legacy of entrepreneurial excellence in the textile industry, we bring a deep-rooted understanding of long-term value, integrity, and growth into the fast-paced world of property.

At our core, we are a boutique agency with a bold spirit — blending personalized service with market intelligence to guide our clients toward the right investment, the right home, and the right future.

We are more than agents. We are advisors, connectors, and curators of spaces that hold meaning and potential. Whether you're buying, selling, renting, or investing, we stand beside you every step of the way with care, clarity, and confidence.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Dubai’s most trusted boutique real estate agency, known for creating meaningful client relationships, delivering unmatched market insight, and helping every client build a lifestyle and legacy through property.

Our Mission

To simplify and elevate the real estate journey by offering expert guidance, personalized service, and long-term value.

Our Services

Property Sales (Secondary Market)

We help clients buy and sell ready-to-move-in properties across Dubai’s most sought-after communities. Our deep understanding of the market ensures every transaction is strategic, smooth, and rewarding.

Off-Plan Investments

From early-stage launches to exclusive allocations, we assist investors in securing high-potential properties directly from Dubai’s top developers — offering tailored advice based on budget, ROI, and future goals.

Luxury Rentals

We match high-quality tenants with premium rental properties. Whether you’re relocating or upgrading, our rental experts ensure a seamless, end-to-end leasing experience.

Real Estate Advisory

We offer personalized consultation services to guide first-time buyers, seasoned investors, and property owners on the best real estate decisions — from market entry to portfolio management.

Marketing & Listing Support

With professional photography, videography, and listing strategy, we present your property in its best light. Our in-house creative team ensures maximum visibility and interest, across all major platforms.
